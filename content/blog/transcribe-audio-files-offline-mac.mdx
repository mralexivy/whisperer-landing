## How It Works

<StepList>
<Step num="1" title="Open File Transcription">
Click the Whisperer icon in your menu bar and select **File Transcription** from the sidebar.
</Step>

<Step num="2" title="Drop your file">
Drag and drop a file onto the drop zone, or click **Choose File** to browse your Mac. You can transcribe files of any length.
</Step>

<Step num="3" title="Processing happens locally">
Whisperer runs the file through your selected Whisper model. Everything stays on your Mac.
</Step>

<Step num="4" title="Get your text">
Once complete, the full transcription appears below the drop zone. Copy it to your clipboard or save it to your transcription history.
</Step>
</StepList>

<Callout type="tip">
**Speed depends on your model.** Turbo is often faster than real-time on Apple Silicon. Large V3 is slower but better for difficult audio.
</Callout>

## Accuracy

Same Whisper models as live dictation. Results depend on:

- **Model size** — Large V3 is most accurate, Turbo is fastest
- **Audio quality** — clear recordings work best
- **Language** — 100+ languages, accuracy varies
- **Dictionary** — add custom terms for specialized vocabulary

<Callout type="info">
For technical content: Large V3 model plus domain terms in your [personal dictionary](/blog/personal-dictionary-setup).
</Callout>
