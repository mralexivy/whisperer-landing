## How It Works

<StepList>
<Step num="1" title="Open File Transcription">
Click the Whisperer icon in your menu bar and select **File Transcription** from the sidebar.
</Step>

<Step num="2" title="Drop your file">
Drag and drop a file onto the drop zone, or click **Choose File** to browse your Mac. You can transcribe files of any length.
</Step>

<Step num="3" title="AI processing happens locally">
Whisperer processes the file using your selected Whisper model. Everything runs on-device — your audio is never uploaded anywhere.
</Step>

<Step num="4" title="Get your text">
Once complete, the full transcription appears below the drop zone. Copy it to your clipboard or save it to your transcription history.
</Step>
</StepList>

<Callout type="tip">
**Processing speed depends on your model choice.** The Turbo model transcribes fastest — often faster than real-time on Apple Silicon Macs. Large V3 is slower but more accurate for difficult audio (heavy accents, background noise, technical vocabulary).
</Callout>

## Use Cases

### Meeting Recordings

Record your Zoom, Google Meet, or in-person meetings, then transcribe them offline. No cloud transcription service needed — and no privacy concerns about sending meeting audio to third parties.

### Podcast & Interview Transcription

Transcribe podcast episodes, interview recordings, or research conversations. Useful for journalists, researchers, and content creators who need searchable text from audio.

### Voice Memos

Transcribe iPhone Voice Memos that sync to your Mac via iCloud. Great for turning on-the-go thoughts into searchable text.

### Lecture Notes

Students can record lectures and transcribe them offline for study notes. The personal dictionary helps with field-specific terminology.

## Accuracy

File transcription uses the same Whisper models as live dictation. Accuracy depends on:

- **Model size** — Large V3 is the most accurate; Turbo is fastest
- **Audio quality** — clear recordings with minimal background noise transcribe best
- **Language** — Whisper supports 100+ languages with varying accuracy levels
- **Personal dictionary** — add custom terms to improve recognition of specialized vocabulary

<Callout type="info">
For best results with technical content, use the Large V3 model and add domain-specific terms to your [personal dictionary](/blog/personal-dictionary-setup).
</Callout>
