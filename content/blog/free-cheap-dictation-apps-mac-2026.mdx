Looking for a dictation app that won't break the bank? The Mac dictation market has options ranging from free to $249. This guide covers every affordable option — apps under $30 — and shows you what you get at each price point.

Everything above costs less than the price of lunch. Compare that to Superwhisper ($249), Voibe ($99), or Wispr Flow ($120+/year) — and the value difference becomes obvious.

## 1. Apple Dictation — Free

**Price:** Free | **Offline:** Yes (Apple Silicon) | **Best for:** Casual dictation

Apple's built-in Dictation has improved significantly with macOS Sequoia and Tahoe. It runs on-device on Apple Silicon Macs and supports basic voice-to-text in any app.

<FeatureGrid>
<FeatureCard title="What's Good" icon="star">
Free. No setup. Works everywhere. On-device processing on Apple Silicon. Decent accuracy for conversational English.
</FeatureCard>
<FeatureCard title="What's Missing" icon="x">
No Code Mode. No per-app profiles. Aggressive autocorrection ruins technical terms. No personal dictionary. No file transcription. No transcription history.
</FeatureCard>
</FeatureGrid>

**Verdict:** Apple Dictation is fine for quick notes and messages. But if you write code, use technical terms, or need any customization, you'll outgrow it quickly.

## 2. Whisperer — $2.99 / $14.99

**Price:** $2.99 base, $14.99 Pro Pack lifetime | **Offline:** Yes | **Best for:** Developers, power users, anyone who wants full features at the lowest price

Whisperer is a menu-bar dictation app that inserts text into any focused field. The Pro Pack adds Code Mode (camelCase, snake_case, symbols by voice), per-app profiles, personal dictionary, and AI post-processing.

<Callout type="tip">
At $14.99 lifetime, Whisperer Pro is the most feature-rich dictation app under $30. It includes capabilities that competitors charge $99–$249 for — Code Mode, per-app profiles, three transcription engines, and AI post-processing with 10 modes.
</Callout>

### What $2.99 Gets You (Base)

- Hold-to-record and toggle dictation
- 3 transcription engines (Whisper, Parakeet, Apple Speech)
- 100+ languages
- Live streaming preview
- File transcription (audio and video)
- Filler word removal
- Transcription history with search
- 100% offline

### What $14.99 Gets You (Pro Pack)

Everything in Base, plus:
- **Code Mode** — camelCase, snake_case, PascalCase, symbols by voice
- **Per-app profiles** — automatic mode switching
- **Personal dictionary** — custom terms and names
- **AI Post-Processing** — 10 offline modes (rewrite, translate, summarize, format, grammar, and more)
- **Pro insertion engine** — clipboard-safe paste
- **Usage statistics** — WPM, peak hours, charts

**Verdict:** The best value in Mac dictation. Period. Three unique features (Code Mode, per-app profiles, three engines) that no competitor offers at any price — for $14.99 once.

## 3. JesType — €14.95

**Price:** €14.95 one-time | **Offline:** Yes | **Best for:** Simple offline dictation

JesType is a straightforward offline dictation app with a clean interface. It supports Whisper and Moonshine models for on-device transcription.

<FeatureGrid>
<FeatureCard title="What's Good" icon="star">
Simple UX. Fully offline. Moonshine model support. Similar price to Whisperer. Good SEO landing page for "offline dictation Mac."
</FeatureCard>
<FeatureCard title="What's Missing" icon="x">
No Code Mode. No per-app profiles. No AI post-processing. No file transcription. No transcription history. Single engine focus.
</FeatureCard>
</FeatureGrid>

**Verdict:** A competent basic dictation app. But at the same price as Whisperer Pro, you get significantly fewer features. Worth considering if you value extreme simplicity over capability.

## 4. VoiceInk — ~$25

**Price:** ~$25 one-time | **Offline:** Yes | **Best for:** Budget Mac dictation with clean design

VoiceInk is a Mac dictation app focused on simplicity and good design. It uses Whisper for on-device transcription.

**Verdict:** A solid mid-range option. More expensive than Whisperer with fewer features, but well-designed and reliable for basic dictation needs.

## 5. Spokenly — Free + Premium

**Price:** Free tier + premium | **Offline:** Partial | **Best for:** Users who want free with iOS

Spokenly offers a free tier and iOS companion app. It's growing quickly but still maturing on features.

**Verdict:** Good free option if you need iOS dictation. For Mac-specific features, Whisperer offers more depth at $14.99 lifetime.
