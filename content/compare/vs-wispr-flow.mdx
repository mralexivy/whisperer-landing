Wispr Flow and Whisperer take fundamentally different approaches to dictation. Wispr Flow uses cloud-based AI to rewrite and format your speech. Whisperer processes everything [offline on your Mac](/offline-dictation-mac/). Which approach is right for you?

## The Core Difference

**Whisperer:** Your voice is recorded, transcribed by a local model, and inserted into the active app. Nothing leaves your Mac. Ever.

**Wispr Flow:** Your voice is sent to cloud servers for transcription and AI processing. The cloud AI reformats your speech into polished text.

This isn't a minor distinction — it affects privacy, reliability, speed, cost, and whether the app works without internet.

## Where Whisperer Wins

<FeatureCard title="Speed — No Cloud Latency" icon="zap">

### Three Transcription Engines
Whisperer offers [Whisper, Parakeet, and Apple Speech](/features/offline-transcription/) — hot-swappable without restarting. Wispr Flow uses a single cloud backend. Whisperer's Parakeet engine on the Neural Engine is the fastest option available on Apple Silicon.

### AI Post-Processing (Offline)
Whisperer includes [10 AI modes](/features/ai-writing/) — Rewrite, Translate, Format, Summarize, Grammar, List Format, Coding, Email, Creative, Custom — all running offline. Not as powerful as GPT-4, but runs without internet and has zero privacy concerns.

## Where Wispr Flow Wins

### AI Rewriting Quality
Wispr Flow's cloud AI leverages GPT-class models for text transformation. This produces higher-quality rewriting than on-device models. If you dictate stream-of-consciousness and want polished prose output, Wispr Flow's cloud AI is genuinely better at this specific task.

### Cross-Platform (Mac + Windows)
Wispr Flow supports Mac and Windows. Whisperer is Mac-only. If you switch between platforms, Wispr Flow is the only option.

### Vibe Coding Positioning
Wispr Flow has positioned itself well in the "vibe coding" narrative, with features and content targeting AI-assisted development workflows.

## The Privacy Question

<Callout type="warning">
When you dictate with Wispr Flow, consider what you're sending to cloud servers:
- **Source code** and technical architecture discussions
- **Business emails** and confidential communications
- **Client names**, project details, financial figures
- **Passwords** or sensitive information mentioned verbally
- **Medical**, legal, or personal information

With Whisperer, none of this leaves your device. Your Mac is the only infrastructure involved.
</Callout>

## Who Should Choose What

<Callout type="tip">
**Choose Whisperer if:**
- Privacy matters — 100% offline, no data leaves your Mac
- You're a developer — Code Mode is essential and unique
- Budget matters — $14.99 once vs $120–180/year
- You need offline reliability — works without internet
- You want per-app profiles — automatic mode switching

**Choose Wispr Flow if:**
- You need GPT-4-class AI rewriting and privacy is less of a concern
- You need Windows support
- You're willing to pay $10–15/month for cloud AI quality
- You don't need Code Mode or per-app profiles
</Callout>
