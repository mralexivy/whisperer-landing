Spokenly offers free Mac dictation with paid upgrades. It's relatively new and growing. Whisperer costs money upfront but runs fully offline with developer features Spokenly doesn't have.

## Price Comparison

| | Whisperer | Spokenly |
|---|-----------|----------|
| Free tier | Free trial | Yes |
| Base app | $2.99 one-time | Free |
| Pro version | $14.99 lifetime | Premium (varies) |
| Subscription | No | Premium tiers may apply |
| Cloud fees | None (100% offline) | Possible |

<Callout type="info">
Spokenly has a free tier. Whisperer has a free trial but then costs $2.99 base or $14.99 for Pro. Whisperer is one-time; Spokenly's premium tiers may be recurring.
</Callout>

## Where Whisperer Wins

<FeatureGrid>
<FeatureCard title="Code Mode" icon="code">
Whisperer is the only Mac dictation app with Code Mode. Dictate `camelCase`, `snake_case`, parentheses, brackets, semicolons by voice. Spokenly doesn't have developer features.
</FeatureCard>

<FeatureCard title="Fully Offline" icon="lock">
Everything runs on your Mac. No cloud, no data leaving your machine. Matters if you dictate proprietary code or sensitive info.
</FeatureCard>

<FeatureCard title="Three Engines" icon="cpu">
Whisper (GPU), NVIDIA (Neural Engine), Apple Speech. Switch between them without restarting. Spokenly has one backend.
</FeatureCard>

<FeatureCard title="Per-App Profiles" icon="target">
Different apps, different behavior. Code Mode in VS Code, normal text in Slack. Spokenly is the same everywhere.
</FeatureCard>
</FeatureGrid>

## Where Spokenly Wins

### Free Tier
Spokenly is free to start. Whisperer has a trial, then costs money. If free is the main criterion, Spokenly wins.

### iOS App
Spokenly has an iPhone/iPad companion. Whisperer is Mac only.

### Active Development
Spokenly is newer and growing. Worth keeping an eye on as it matures.

## Bottom Line

<Callout type="tip">
**Developers:** Whisperer. Code Mode doesn't exist in Spokenly.

**Budget:** Spokenly is free. Whisperer is $2.99-14.99 one-time.

**Privacy:** Whisperer runs offline. No audio leaves your Mac.

**iOS:** Spokenly has a mobile app. Whisperer doesn't.
</Callout>
